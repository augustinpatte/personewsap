# PersoNewsAP Product Brief

## Product Vision

PersoNewsAP is a premium daily education app for ambitious 18-25 year-old students. It helps users understand the world, build sharper judgment, and prepare for ambitious careers through one focused daily learning drop.

The product is not a feed. It is a 5-minute daily ritual: concise, useful, sourced, and designed to compound over time.

## Target User

PersoNewsAP is built for ambitious students and young adults across all fields:

- Business, finance, law, medicine, engineering, humanities, media, sport, and tech students.
- Early-career builders who want to sound informed and think clearly.
- Users who want practical context without doomscrolling.
- Users who prefer one strong daily update over endless content.

The user is busy, intelligent, and impatient with generic summaries. They want signal, relevance, and direct explanations.

## Core Value Proposition

PersoNewsAP gives users one daily update that makes them sharper in five minutes.

Each day, the app provides:

- A personalized AI-generated newsletter based on user preferences.
- One business story that explains how the world works.
- One practical mini-case challenge to train decision-making.
- One key concept to build long-term knowledge.

The experience should feel premium, direct, and useful: less like a news feed, more like a private daily briefing for ambitious students.

## Daily Experience

The Today screen contains one daily drop only.

The daily drop includes:

1. Personalized AI newsletter
2. Business story
3. Practical mini-case challenge
4. Key concept

The user can complete the drop, save items, give feedback, and return later through the Library. The Today experience must never become an infinite feed or a scroll trap.

## Onboarding Preferences

Onboarding collects the minimum needed to personalize the daily drop:

- Language: `fr` or `en`
- Topics:
  - `business`
  - `finance`
  - `tech_ai`
  - `law`
  - `medicine`
  - `engineering`
  - `sport_business`
  - `culture_media`
- Articles per topic: 1 to 3
- Frequency: daily by default
- Goal:
  - `understand_world`
  - `prepare_career`
  - `learn_business`
  - `explore_stem`
  - `become_sharper_daily`

Preferences should be editable after onboarding.

## Content Modules

### Personalized AI Newsletter

A daily selection of short, sourced articles based on the user's language, selected topics, article counts, and goal.

### Business Story

One concise story explaining a company, market, strategy, founder decision, failure, turnaround, or economic mechanism.

### Practical Mini-Case Challenge

One short decision-making exercise. The user receives context, a question, and a practical challenge. The goal is to practice thinking, not pass an exam.

### Key Concept

One concept users can apply in conversations, interviews, school, internships, or personal projects. It may appear daily or every two days depending on content quality and pacing.

## Non-Goals

- No infinite feed.
- No social network.
- No public comments at launch.
- No creator marketplace.
- No real-time breaking news dependency.
- No low-quality AI summaries without sources.
- No literal FR/EN translation as the main content strategy.
- No paid plan at launch unless explicitly re-scoped.
- No per-user content generation at app open.

## MVP Scope

The MVP should include:

- Expo React Native iOS/Android app.
- Supabase auth.
- Onboarding and editable preferences.
- One Today daily drop.
- Four content modules.
- Library for past daily drops.
- FR/EN support.
- Sourced and versioned content stored in Supabase.
- Basic feedback and completion tracking.
- Admin or script-based generation pipeline.
- Free product at launch.

## Future Scope

Future versions may include:

- Push notification personalization.
- Streaks and lightweight progress.
- Saved concepts and personal knowledge library.
- Mini-case answer evaluation.
- Better goal-based personalization.
- Editorial review dashboard.
- School or cohort-specific drops.
- Premium tiers after product-market fit.
- Audio version of daily drops.
- Calendar-based exam or internship preparation tracks.
